Each agent will be messaged automatically when you save a change to their schedule today. Set up one webhook per person pointing to their Slack DM.

How to get a webhook URL (repeat for each person):
  1. Go to api.slack.com/apps and open your existing app (or create a new one)
  2. Click "Incoming Webhooks" → "Add New Webhook to Workspace"
  3. Search for the person's name to send to their DM, or pick a channel
  4. Copy the webhook URL and paste it in the field above
